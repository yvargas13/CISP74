"Be Heard, Be Seen"

In coalition to make film viewing accessible to deaf and visually impaired individuals who love movie-going, this is a database to collect film requests who would like to have an audio description. Our services would include a digital copy of the film sent directly via email with the requested services.

For further assistance and clarity on which films DO NOT have audio descriptions available, please refer to The Audio Description Project.
